# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Tone**: Encouraging, professional, and clear.
- **Clarity**: Use simple language to explain complex programming concepts (like Fizz-Buzz).
- **Engagement**: Use active voice to keep the player motivated.
- **Precision**: Ensure technical terms are used accurately but explained when necessary.

## Branding
- **Name**: "Fizz Buzz" (two words, capitalized).
- **Logo/Visuals**: Clean, modern, and high-contrast. Use a "Retro UI" aesthetic (as suggested by the skill name `frontend-design` in the project).
- **Color Palette**: Bold colors with a focus on readability and accessibility.

## UX Principles
- **Speed First**: Minimal loading times and instantaneous feedback for player actions.
- **Mobile Optimization**: Touch-friendly interface with clear, large interactive elements.
- **Persistence**: Game state and history should be saved automatically and seamlessly.
- **Feedback**: Provide clear visual and auditory (if applicable) cues for correct and incorrect answers.
- **Accessibility**: Ensure high color contrast and screen-reader friendly elements.

## Design Philosophy
- **Modern-Retro**: A bold, Neo-Brutalist look that feels both classic and cutting-edge.
- **Simplicity**: Focus on the core gameplay loop without unnecessary distractions.
- **Transparency**: Make it clear how the local-first storage works for users interested in the technical side.
